GOOD HEALTH AND WELL-BEING

United Nations Sustainable Development Goal 3 (Philippines)

Intro Goal Issues PH Context Programs Action

Introduction

SDG 3 aims to ensure healthy lives and promote well-being for all at all ages. Health is essential to human progress and sustainable development. Ensuring access to quality healthcare, vaccines, and nutrition contributes to a healthier and more resilient society.

Goal

The goal of SDG 3 is to reduce maternal and child mortality, fight communicable diseases, promote mental health, and achieve universal health coverage. It also emphasizes access to essential medicines, vaccines, and clean environments.

Key Health Challenges

  • Limited access to healthcare in rural and remote areas.
  • Rising cases of lifestyle-related diseases like diabetes and hypertension.
  • High rates of maternal and infant mortality in low-income communities.
  • Mental health stigma and lack of support systems.
  • Insufficient vaccination coverage and health education.

Philippine Situation

Government Programs

  • Universal Health Care (UHC) Act: Ensures that every Filipino has access to affordable and quality healthcare services.
  • DOH Health Promotion Framework Strategy: Focuses on preventive healthcare and healthy lifestyles.
  • National Immunization Program (NIP): Provides free vaccines to children and vulnerable groups.
  • National Mental Health Program: Promotes mental health awareness and services nationwide.

Take Action

You can contribute to SDG 3 by maintaining a healthy lifestyle, getting vaccinated, supporting health campaigns, and promoting mental health awareness. Small actions like exercise, proper hygiene, and empathy can build a healthier, happier nation.
